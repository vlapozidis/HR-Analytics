# HR Analytics Dashboard – Power BI  

This project is an **HR Analytics Dashboard** built in Power BI to analyze employee data, attrition, and workforce insights.  
The goal is to empower HR and management teams with actionable insights that support decision-making around employee retention, engagement, and compensation.  

---

## 📌 Dashboard Sections  

### 1. Top KPIs (Cards)  
- Total Employees  
- Attrition %  
- Average Monthly Income  
- Average Tenure (Years at Company)  

### 2. Attrition Analysis  
- Attrition Rate by Department & Job Role (stacked bar)  
- Attrition by AgeGroup (column)  
- Attrition by SalarySlab (column)  
- Attrition vs. Overtime (clustered bar)  

### 3. Demographics  
- Gender Distribution (donut)  
- Marital Status (donut)  
- Education Field (donut/pie)  
- Age Distribution (histogram)  

### 4. Compensation & Growth  
- Monthly Income by Job Level (box/column)  
- Monthly Income by Education Field (bar)  
- Salary Hike % by Department (bar)  

### 5. Tenure & Career Progression  
- Years at Company Distribution (histogram)  
- Years Since Last Promotion (column)  
- Years in Current Role vs Attrition (bar)  

---

## ⚡ Features  
- Fully interactive dashboard with slicers & filters (Department, Role, AgeGroup, Overtime).  
- KPI Cards at the top for quick at-a-glance insights.  
- Consistent design with a professional HR analytics theme.  

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used  
- Power BI  
- DAX Measures (for Attrition %, Avg. Income, Avg. Tenure)  
- Dataset: HR Analytics (1,480 employees, 38 fields)
